Why business interruption insurance?

It's pretty simple.
Small businesses are particularly vulnerable to cyber events as a result of two phenomena.

  • First, most small businesses, as a result of their size, don’t keep large amounts of cash on hand. They can’t afford to.
  • Second, e-commerce is an increasingly important source of revenue for small businesses.

This is a dangerous combination. If a small business suffers a cyber event that prevents them from digitally collecting revenue, they are faced with the prospect of being unable to make payroll or pay their most critical bills. Their very survival is at stake.

That’s where we come in.
