Where resilience meets e-commerce

CyberFortress is an insurtech startup based in San Antonio, TX. We've built a new kind of insurance policy designed for e-commerce businesses and virtual storefronts. Narrowly-focused and parametric in nature, our policy pays out within 24 hours of an unexpected downtime event - providing valuable cash-on-hand to help our customers weather the immediate storm.

Meet the Team

Over the past decade, our team has helped tens of thousands small businesses with their IT and cybersecurity needs. Our backgrounds include managing portfolio risk at Bridgewater Associates, the world's largest hedge fund; running web-scale infrastructure at Rackspace, a leading cloud technology provider that was obsessed with customer experience; and launching innovative insurtechs from Lemonade, a disruptive insurance startup that is changing the way consumers think about and consume insurance, to Tesla Insurance, transforming the business model of traditional auto insurance.
